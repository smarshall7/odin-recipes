Lemon Cake In A Mug

How to make a delicious lemon cake quickly in your microwave

Have you ever craved a little sweet treat? This is the perfect recipe to get your sweet fix. This recipe is simple, fast, and calorie conscious. Enjoy your lemony treat today!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup +1 ½ teaspoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • dash salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 3 tablespoon milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon lemon curd

Steps

  1. Add flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt to a mug and stir together.
  2. Stir in milk, lemon curd, melted butter, and vanilla extract until smooth, being sure to scrape the bottom of the mug.
  3. Cook in microwave for 70-90 seconds* (until cake is just set, but still barely shiny on top). Allow to rest in microwave for 1 minute before consuming.
